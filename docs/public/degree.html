Degree overview

Purpose

The goal of the computer engineering degree is to produce individuals with a strong background in science and technology who can design, develop, implement, and maintain computer systems, including both hardware and software. It seeks to provide students with the skills they need to take charge of projects, adapt to changes in technology, and effectively and independently solve challenging problems.

This degree adopts a comprehensive approach, encompassing every facet of computer science, from distributed systems and networks to cybersecurity and artificial intelligence. It integrates technological, scientific, ethical, and management knowledge to prepare students to face the challenges of the ICT industry.To ensure that graduates are equipped to continue learning and developing in a rapidly changing field while maintaining a high employability rate, the degree ultimately aims to create information and communication technology specialists who can drive digital transformation in businesses and organizations.
